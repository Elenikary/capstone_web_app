/Eleni Karyofylli/



My name is Eleni Karyofylli and I am currently a senior at Lehigh University studying Electrical Engineering and Product Design.
I am originally from Thessaloniki, Greece and moved to the US for my undergraduate degrees.
Upon graduation, I aspire to work in the Engineering Design industry of electronic or other products.
I am truly fascinated by all aspects of the product development process and I am looking for opportunities
to apply my skills and creativity in product design and consulting.
